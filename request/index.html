Request a Newbie Guide

If you like our guides and would like one created for your own community, we're here to help.

Simply send an email to samegavi@gmail.com with the following:

Subject: Newbie Guide Needed by [your community name]

Important: Please include your contact number in the message body.

We'll review your request and get back to you as soon as possible to discuss how we can create a customized guide that meets your community's specific needs.

Our guides are designed to be simple, accessible, and helpful for newcomers to quickly understand and navigate your community.
