Dear Book Enthusiast,

Welcome to !ABC, where our passion for books goes beyond just reading them. We're a vibrant community dedicated to discovering and inspiring young readers while celebrating the material life of books across generations.

What Makes !ABC Different

Unlike traditional book clubs, !ABC focuses on:

  • Fostering a love of reading in young people
  • Appreciating books as physical objects with history and character
  • Creating connections between readers of all ages
  • Offering hands-on experiences through workshops and trips

What We Offer

  • Interactive Workshops: From bookbinding to storytelling techniques
  • Inspiring Seminars: Featuring authors, illustrators, and publishing experts
  • Engaging Talks: Discussions about literature's impact across generations
  • Literary Excursions: Visits to bookshops, libraries, and literary landmarks
